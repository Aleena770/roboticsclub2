Our Mission

Our mission is to harness imagination, collaboration, and curiosity to make innovation a reality for many. As part of this team, every member is encouraged to question, experiment, and develop as we create and construct robots that evaluate our abilities while offering fresh concepts to the table. We aren't just here to solely compete; we're here to cultivate an atmosphere where fun education and exploration are the driving forces behind what we do.

The Robotics Society was founded by a dedicated group of college students who wanted to expand their knowledge on how technology can be used to tackle everyday issues. Over time, we developed a team that was devoted to creating, building, and programming modern robots after a year of small group projects during our free time. As our club expanded over the years, we ended up creating a community that promotes technicality, creativity, and curiosity; each project, therefore, is an opportunity to grow, learn, and enhance our concepts to the best of our abilities.

Projects

Our projects were made to help us solve issues in the real world by applying what we learn through our educational upbringing. Every building session enables us to collaborate as a team, test out the newest technology out there, as well as gain programming experience. With the help of these projects, we hope to gain hands-on knowledge that will strengthen our education and have us be prepared for the technical opportunities that await us in the future.

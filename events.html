Events

We provide a variety of fun and exciting events to students all year round. From workshops, to hackathons, and networking events.

Our Schedule

Workshops/Hackathons

MechaWorks Workshop

A hands-on session where students learn the fundamentals of building and wiring small robotic systems.

QuantLab Robotics Workshop

A guided technical lab focused on sensors, coding basics, and testing simple autonomous behaviours.

RoboRush Hackathon

A fast-paced 12-hour challenge where teams design and program a functional robot from scratch.

MechaSprint Hackathon

A competitive build-and-code event pushing students to create innovative robotic solutions under time pressure.
